All About Tortellini

Tortellini has been produced by traditional means for hundreds of years in Italy

In modern times tortellini has been mass-produced for the public, available in global supermarkets

Tortellini is loved by chefs and epicureans due to its flexibility: it has an unlimited combination of fillings

Tortellini can be paired with a range of fine Italian wines, depending on its fillings and sauce

Tortellini has been a constant theme throughout my life. Some of my earliest happy memories involve tortellini. Tortellini was one of the first dishes I learned to hand-prepare. Now I serve tortellini to my family. It really is a wonderous dish

Nick Thomas, world-renowned tortellini consumer
